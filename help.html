Account Fields

  • title
  • accounts
  • accounts.default

All other fields are represented in accounts.default:

  • identity
  • identity.seed
  • identity.characterRepertoire
  • identity.start
  • identity.length
  • identity.shift
  • identity.inserts
  • identity.inserts[].value
  • identity.inserts[].position
  • display
  • display.name
  • display.url
  • display.user
  • display.user.name
  • display.user.url
